Accommodation

We know that most of you will be travelling to join us for the big day, and we're so grateful for you making the trip - it really means a lot!

There is only a small amount of accommodation available at the venue itsels. If we've not already been in touch with you about staying on site, then unfortunately there's no room at the inn! But there are lots of alternative options available nearby (links in the accommodation names).

Please make sure to book your taxis for the end of the night so your dancing feet can get some rest!
